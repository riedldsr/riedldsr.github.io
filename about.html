About

As a child, I was an avid reader of any fantasy book I could get my hands on. Not necessarily for their tales of heroes and villains, but for the monsters and animals that dwelt between the pages.

If it had animals in it, I was all about it. I have always had a sort of reverence for animals, and that has helped guide my artistic works. I have spent countless hours studying animal anatomy, re-drawing poses until I got them just right.

Within the pages of this website, I hope you can explore and appreciate some of the cryptid animals I love so much. Dragons, basilisks, Mothman, Nessie, even Cthulu, all hold a special place in my heart.

This is the beginning to a life-long dream of selling my art to others, to bring them joy and tell them a story with my works.

I hope you truly enjoy the dive into my overly-imaginative mind.
